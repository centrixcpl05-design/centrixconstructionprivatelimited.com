About Us

Centrix Construction Private Limited is a professionally managed construction, civil engineering, and interior solutions company based in Delhi NCR, delivering reliable and high-quality construction services for residential, commercial, and infrastructure projects.

We specialize in complete construction solutions including residential construction, commercial projects, RCC structural work, renovation & remodeling, interior finishing, flooring, masonry, waterproofing, steel fabrication, and project management services. Our company is committed to maintaining the highest standards of workmanship, structural durability, safety, and modern construction practices in every project.

Our approach is built on transparent communication, professional execution, proper planning, and client-focused project management. We believe in delivering cost-effective solutions with clear timelines, quality materials, and systematic supervision to ensure smooth workflow and long-term project performance.

With a skilled workforce, experienced supervisors, and dedicated project coordination, we successfully manage both small-scale and large-scale residential and commercial developments with precision, efficiency, and attention to detail. From foundation work to final finishing, we focus on delivering projects that combine strength, functionality, and premium finishing standards.

Our Mission: To provide durable, high-quality, and cost-effective construction solutions with complete customer satisfaction, professional integrity, and timely project execution.

Our Vision: To become a trusted and leading construction company recognized for excellence, innovation, reliability, and long-term value in the construction and infrastructure industry.

Our Core Services

1. Residential Construction (Turnkey Work)

We provide complete residential construction services from foundation to final finishing. Our turnkey solutions include excavation, RCC structure, brickwork, plastering, flooring, electrical work, plumbing, painting, modular installations, and final handover. We focus on quality materials, modern designs, timely completion, and customer satisfaction for villas, independent houses, duplexes, and residential buildings.

2. Commercial Construction (Custom Projects)

We undertake commercial construction projects including shops, offices, showrooms, warehouses, and commercial complexes with customized layouts and professional planning. Our services include structural work, partition systems, false ceiling, flooring, electrical setup, front elevation, and interior finishing designed to meet modern business requirements and functional spaces.

3. RCC Work (Structural Work)

We specialize in reinforced cement concrete (RCC) work for strong and durable structures. Our services include footing, columns, beams, slabs, staircases, retaining walls, and complete structural frameworks. We ensure proper steel reinforcement, shuttering, concrete mixing, vibration, and curing according to engineering standards for maximum strength and safety.

4. Renovation & Remodeling (Upgrade Work)

We provide renovation and remodeling solutions for residential and commercial properties to improve appearance, functionality, and structural condition. Our services include wall redesign, flooring replacement, false ceiling installation, bathroom and kitchen renovation, painting, repair work, and complete interior upgrades to give old spaces a modern and elegant look.

5. Interior & Finishing (Interior Work)

Our interior and finishing services include premium painting, putty work, texture finishes, false ceiling, gypsum partitions, modular furniture, lighting arrangements, wallpaper installation, and decorative interiors. We create stylish and functional spaces with attention to detail, modern aesthetics, and high-quality finishing standards for homes and commercial spaces.

6. Flooring & Tiling (Floor Work)

We offer professional flooring and tiling solutions using ceramic tiles, vitrified tiles, marble, granite, kota stone, and designer flooring materials. Our team ensures proper surface leveling, alignment, cutting, grouting, and finishing for durable, elegant, and long-lasting flooring installations suitable for residential and commercial projects.

7. Plastering & Masonry (Civil Work)

Our plastering and masonry services include brickwork, block work, internal and external plastering, wall construction, repair work, and finishing applications. We ensure smooth surfaces, accurate alignment, strong bonding, and durable wall structures with quality workmanship suitable for all types of civil construction projects.

8. Waterproofing (Protection Work)

We provide advanced waterproofing solutions to prevent leakage, seepage, dampness, and water damage. Our services cover terrace waterproofing, bathroom treatment, basement protection, wall coating, crack filling, and chemical applications using modern waterproofing materials for long-term durability and protection of buildings and structures.

9. Steel & Fabrication (Steel Work)

We undertake steel fabrication and structural metal work including bar bending, cutting, welding, railing work, gates, sheds, staircases, industrial structures, and custom fabrication projects. Our team ensures precision work, strong joints, durable finishing, and safe structural installations using high-quality steel materials and modern fabrication techniques.

10. Project Management (Execution Control)

We provide complete project management services including site supervision, labor coordination, material planning, budgeting, quality control, timeline monitoring, and execution management. Our goal is to ensure smooth workflow, cost-effective construction, timely delivery, and high-quality results for every residential and commercial project.
