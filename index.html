About Centrix Construction Private limited

Centrix Construction Pvt Ltd is a fast-growing construction and contracting company focused on delivering high-quality civil engineering and labour-based solutions across residential, commercial, and government projects. Founded with a vision to build reliable infrastructure and long-term client relationships, the company operates with strong leadership, practical execution, and financial discipline. Our expertise includes PCC (Plain Cement Concrete) work, labour contracting, structural construction, and government tender execution. We ensure every project is completed with precision, cost control, and strict adherence to timelines. With a dedicated team and hands-on management approach, Centrix Construction Pvt Ltd aims to establish itself as a trusted name in the construction industry.
