About Centrix Construction Pvt Ltd

Centrix Construction Pvt Ltd is a fast-growing construction and contracting company focused on delivering high-quality civil engineering and labour-based solutions across residential, commercial, and government projects.

Founded with a vision to build reliable infrastructure and long-term client relationships, the company operates with strong leadership, practical execution, and financial discipline.

Our expertise includes maintenance, contracting, structural construction, and private & government tender execution. We ensure every project is completed with precision, cost control, and strict adherence to timelines.

With a dedicated team and hands-on management approach, Centrix Construction Pvt Ltd aims to establish itself as a trusted name in the construction industry.
