About Company

Centrix Construction Private Limited is a professionally managed construction and interior solutions company based in Delhi NCR.

We specialize in delivering high-quality civil work, renovation, and interior services with a strong focus on durability, precision finishing, and timely execution.

Our approach is built on clear communication with clients, transparent pricing structures, and consistent quality control throughout the project lifecycle.

With a skilled workforce and structured supervision, we successfully handle both residential and commercial projects, ensuring reliable outcomes and long-term performance.

Our Strengths

Clean and professional work execution standards
